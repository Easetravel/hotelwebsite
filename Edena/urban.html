DESCRIPTION

Urban accommodates up to 6 people. If you are wanting to explore Boston, the subway entrance is only a couple of minutes walk or there is bicycle rental at the front of the building. Your unit includes 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, lounge area and full kitchen facilities. Additionally in your unit we provide a TV with Netflix subscription, High-speed Wifi and the room boasts amazing views of Boston through its grand french windows. In the building there is a whole foods for your convenience as well as a gym, pool, cafe, library, rooftop terrace, bar and restaurants.

NEIGHBOURHOODS

Urban residences are in the 0.5 mile radius of downtown Boston. Boston downtown and immediate surrounds are home to a constant hustle and bustle of life and activity. You’ll never be without somewhere to eat, shop or sight-see.

Medical centres are closeby, such as, Tufts Hospital (biggest medical center in centre of Boston). Convenient pharmacies and doctors offices are also within a close distance.

Shopping options are in the multitudes with shopping centres such as Macy’s and TJMaxx nearby. Designer shops doors are open with ranges of the newest fashions, including (but not limited to) Hermes, Bottega Venetta, etc

Many international cuisines options are available at a short walking distance from your stay, some of the best Mexican, Middle Eastern, European and Asian restaraunts await your hungry mouths.

Getting Around is a breeze with South Station (main transportation hub for Amtrak and regional buses) only a few minutes walk away and it’s an easy 20 minute drive to and from Boston Logan airport.
